Advanced UI/UX Design

Course Overview

This course is designed for advanced learners who want to master the art of user interface (UI) and user experience (UX) design. You’ll explore in-depth concepts such as design systems, user-centric design processes, accessibility, and advanced prototyping techniques.

What You’ll Learn

  • Creating effective design systems
  • Designing accessible and inclusive user interfaces
  • Conducting advanced user research and testing
  • Using tools like Figma, Sketch, and Adobe XD
  • Prototyping and animations for interactive experiences

Difficulty Level

Advanced
